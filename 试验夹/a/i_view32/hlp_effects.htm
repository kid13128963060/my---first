Effects

 

 Top  Previous  Next

 

 

Click on the Image Menu (with loaded image), then point the mouse at Effects to access these options.

 

Note:Most of these effects can be used on a smaller portion (selection) of an image, as well as the entire image.

These effects are only available, if the PlugIn Effects is installed, which is done at program installation.

 

All actions can be reversed with the Edit Menu's Undo command.

 

 

The possible effects are:

 

3D Button

Click on the Image Menu, point the mouse at Effects and click 3D Button.

 

Gives the image a beveled edge look, with the center raised. The size of the border can be changed using Effects Setup.

 

Blur

Click on the Image Menu, point the mouse at Effects and click Blur.

 

Softens the image, creating a blurry or fuzzy look. The amount of blur can be changed using Effects Setup.

 

Emboss

Click on the Image Menu, point the mouse at Effects and click Emboss.

 

Turns the background grey and gives the image an engraved appearance.

 

Oil Paint

Click on the Image Menu, point the mouse at Effects and click Oil Paint.

 

Creates a textured effect, such as that seen in an oil painting. The amount of texture can be changed using Effects Setup.

 

Edge Detection

Click on the Image Menu, point the mouse at Effects and click Edge Detection.

 

Finds the main lines in an image and replaces their color with the opposite color, at the same time most fill colors are darkened, making them like a background. The image takes on a "painted on velvet" appearance.

 

Median filter

Click on the Image Menu, point the mouse at Effects and click Median filter.

 

Causes a group of pixels to have the brightness of the "average" pixel in that group. In most cases, this has a "smoothing" effect on the area being filtered. The amount of filter can be changed using Effects Setup. The filter can be also used for noise removal e.g. in black/white (1 BPP) images.

 

Explosion

Click on the Image Menu, point the mouse at Effects and click Explosion.

 

Breaks up the image, as if the darker pixels exploded. The amount of explosion can be changed using Effects Setup.

 

Pixelize

Click on the Image Menu, point the mouse at Effects and click Pixelize.

 

Breaks the image into a coarse version of itself, as if it were made up of oversized pixels. The amount can be changed using Effects Setup.

 

 

Other menu options:

 

Effects browser

Click on the Image Menu, point the mouse at Effects and click Effects browser. Opens a dialog from which you can change many of the effects settings.

Info: the Sharpen menu will use the value from the dialog. Default sharpen value is 20.

 

Speech Bubble effect

Click on the Image Menu, point the mouse at Effects and click Insert Speech Bubbles. Opens a dialog from which you can perform the effect. You can insert several bubbles/balloons (using the "Apply to image" button) and later add some text in Edit->Insert text menu.

 

AltaLux effect

Click on the Image Menu, point the mouse at Effects and click AltaLux effect. Opens a dialog from which you can perform the effect. For this feature you must first install the English PlugIn AltaLux.

 

Filter Sandbox

Click on the Image Menu, point the mouse at Effects and click Filter Sandbox. Opens a dialog from which you can perform the effects using JewelScript effects. For this feature you must first install the English PlugIn Filter Sandbox.

 

Film Simulation

Click on the Image Menu, point the mouse at Effects and click Film Simulation. Opens a dialog from which you can perform the effects using the effects. For this feature you must first download and extract the Film Simulation File (irfanview_film_sim_plugin.zip) from the PlugIns website.

 

Filter Factory

Click on the Image Menu, point the mouse at Effects and click Filter Factory. Opens a dialog from which you can perform many other effects. For this feature you must first install the English PlugIn FFactory (Filter Factory).
