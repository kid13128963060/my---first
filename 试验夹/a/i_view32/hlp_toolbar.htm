Toolbar

 

 Top  Previous  Next

 

 

The toolbar allows quick access to some of the most useful features of IrfanView.

 

Turn the toolbar on by using the View Menu and selecting the Show/hide toolbar option. Turn the bar off by again clicking on View and Show/hide toolbar.

 

In the Properties->Toolbar dialog,  you can choose different skins (button designs) or add/remove toolbar buttons.

More toolbar skins are available on the IrfanView homepage.
