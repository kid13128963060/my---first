Swap Colors

 

 Top  Previous  Next

 

 

Click on the Image Menu, then point the mouse at Swap Colors. A sub-menu allows you to select one of the color combinations to change the image.

 

This command operates at the pixel level, changing the color of all, or most, depending on which combination you select.

 

Example:If you choose RGB -> RBG, the pixels that are green will be made blue and the blue pixels will be made green. (In this combination the red pixels stay the same.)

 

The possible swaps are:

 

 RGB -> RBG

 RGB -> BGR

 RGB -> BRG

 RGB -> GRB

 RGB -> GBR
