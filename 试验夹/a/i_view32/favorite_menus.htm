Favorite menus

 

 Top  Previous  Next

 

 

This menu can contain (max 15) favorite menus.

 

You can add a normal menu to Favorites using the right mouse button click on the normal menu.

The click will show a special context menu with options like: Change Menu Hotkey or Add to Favorite Menus.

 

You can delete a single menu from Favorites with the right mouse button click on the favorite menu (or you can clear all favorite menus).
