Negative

 

 Top  Previous  Next

 

 

Click on the Image Menu, then select Negative. The image is converted to a reversed color version of the original.

 

You can invert the whole image or just one of the color channels.

 

Note:The effect can be used on a smaller portion (rectangle selection) of an image, as well as the entire image.

 

This action can be reversed by the Edit Menu's Undo command.
