Cut

 

 Top  Previous  Next

 

 

Use the left mouse button to outline the area (selection), click on the Edit Menu, then Cut (or use the Cut tool on the toolbar).

 

Cut - selection

The area inside the outline will be removed from the image. The area will be usually filled with the black color. The cut portion is placed on the Windows Clipboard as bitmap image and may be pasted in elsewhere.

 

Cut - leave selection

The area outside the outline will be removed from the image and replaced by the defined color for cut area.

 

Note:You can set the cut color in Properties->Browsing/Editing.

 

This action may be reversed with the Edit Menu's Undo command.
