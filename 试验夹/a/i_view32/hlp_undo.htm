Undo - Redo

 

 Top  Previous  Next

 

 

Click on the Edit Menu, select Undo (or use the Undo tool on the toolbar).

 

"Backs up" from the last action you took. Undo will take back many functions of IrfanView. You can disable this option in Browsing/Editing tab in the Options->Properties dialog.

 

Redo will show the Undo image again.

 

Functions supported by Undo are:

Auto adjust colors, Custom Rotation, Convert to Greyscale, Crop, Cut, Decrease Color Depth, Enhance colors, Increase Color Depth, Negative, Resize/Resample, Paint dialog and all Image Effects.
