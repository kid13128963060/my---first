Full Screen

 

 Top  Previous  Next

 

 

Click on the View Menu, then Full Screen. The image will be displayed centered on a black background, which fills the entire screen. Move through the images in the current folder/directory using the space bar or right mouse button for the next image and backspace or left mouse button for previous.

 

To cancel the full screen mode, press the Enter or the ESC key. If you click with left mouse button in the upper left or upper right corner, the full screen mode will also be canceled.

 

The current display option can be changed temporary at any time with the keys 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 and 7.

 

Click the Options Menu, then Properties and finally the Full Screen/Slideshow tab for setting all full screen options.
