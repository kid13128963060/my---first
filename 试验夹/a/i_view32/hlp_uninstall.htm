How to uninstall IrfanView?

 

 

Go to Windows menu: "Start > Programs > IrfanView" and click "Uninstall IrfanView" or see in Windows menu: "Start > Settings > Control Panel > Add/Remove Programs" or "Apps" (if you installed IrfanView using the normal installer).
