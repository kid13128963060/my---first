Convert to Greyscale

 

 Top  Previous  Next

 

 

Click on the Image Menu, then select Convert to Greyscale.

 

Greyscale (or grayscale) is actually a color mode, like RGB. It contains more information about the photo than black and white. Converting from color to greyscale will usually give a better effect than from color to black and white.

 

Note:The effect can be used on a smaller portion (rectangle selection) of an image, as well as the entire image.

 

This action can be reversed by the Edit Menu's Undo command.
