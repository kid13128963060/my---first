Histogram

 

 Top  Previous  Next

 

 

Click on the Image Menu, then Histogram. A dialog appears with the histogram of the image. You can choose which color channel and mode is shown.

 

A click with the right mouse button on the Average values shows a menu which allows you to copy the text value to clipboard.

 

Note:the Histogram dialog is a modeless dialog, so you can leave it opened while you load/browse other files. The dialog will be updated automatically.

 The feature works also with selections: you can create/change the selection in the image and start the dialog or just click on the Update button.

 The dialog can be moved, it will keep the last position.

 

When you want to close the dialog, click on the small X in the dialog corner.
