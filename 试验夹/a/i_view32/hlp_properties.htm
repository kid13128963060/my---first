Properties, Settings

 

 Top  Previous  Next

 

 

Click on the Options Menu, then Properties (or use the Properties tool on the toolbar).

 

A dialog allows you to change many of the basic functions of IrfanView.

 

Click one of the tabs to see the properties in that section. They are:

 

JPEG/PCD/GIF, Extensions, File Handling, Viewing, Full Screen, Video/Sound, Language, Browsing/Editing, Start/Exit, Miscellaneous, PlugIns, Toolbar.
